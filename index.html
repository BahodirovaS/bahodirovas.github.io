    CookEase Recipe Search Web App

    CookEase is a recipe app that was designed and developed using vanilla JS, MongoDB, React-Redux, FastAPI, Bootstrap, and GLightBox. The app is designed to be user-friendly, allowing users to input their dietary or cooking preferences and search for recipes that match their query with the help of a third party API. In addition, logged-in users have the ability to save their favorite recipes to their account for quick access in the future.
